About us

PreOmics is a Series B funded start-up company empowering our clients in life science to establish biological knowledge through efficient, reliable solutions and workflows that set the standard for protein analysis. PreOmics originated from the Matthias Mann Laboratory by developing unique sample preparation kits for protein-based sample analysis. PreOmics iST Kits enable researchers to more quickly analyze and profile their biologic sample from single out of the box solutions. PreOmics complements its sample preparation portfolio through unique automation solutions including the BeatBox and PreON which provide our clients with a complete workflow solution. PreOmics is continuously enhancing the customer workflow experience through our own new instrumentation and applications whilst working with established platform providers.&amp;nbsp;
