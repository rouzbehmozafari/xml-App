About us

Coriolis Pharma, is a globally operating service provider and one of the world leaders in formulation research and development of (bio)pharmaceutical drugs and vaccines.
We revolutionize the development process of biopharmaceutical drugs by integrating innovative digital formulation approaches from drug development to commercial products.

As a people and team-oriented company with an interdisciplinary team of highly skilled scientists and an expert scientific advisory board, we provide cutting-edge services and tailor-made solutions for our clients.

Our company culture is based on diversity, teamwork and open and appreciative communication. That's why wonderful people from over 25 nations around the world work together successfully at Coriolis.

What motivates us? Our motivation is to enable the development of innovative drugs by performing outstanding biopharmaceutical research to improve the quality of life for humankind.

YOUR JOB

  • Analytical characterization of Drug Substance and Drug Product in a GMP regulated environment by using multiple and innovative analytical techniques for analysis of biomolecules like proteins, peptides RNA/DNA and ATMPs
  • Support in experimental planning, laboratory work, data analysis and reporting
  • Internal discussion of data within the project team
  • Taking over responsibility for qualified analytical equipment
  • Support of project teams in qualification and validation activities of equipment and methods
  • Participation in meetings, also with our international customers

WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

  • University degree in a pharmacy or a related field (biotechnology, (bio)chemistry, chemical engineering, biology etc.) &amp;ndash; PhD preferred
  • Ideally work experience in (bio)drug analysis (e.g. HPLC, LO, MFI, qPCR, TCID50, UV) as well as GMP experience in a QC laboratory
  • Team player with strong motivation, inter-personal skills and excellent communication abilities
  • Open for new ideas and tasks, high willingness to learn
