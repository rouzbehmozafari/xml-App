PURPOSE OF YOUR JOB

In the Data Science Unit, we are implementing the scientific IT infrastructure to match our future ambitions within the field of data science. The solution includes a LIMS, automated data parsers for simple and analytical instruments, and a data visualization tool. As we are approaching the operational phase, we are now looking to hire a Data Visualization Specialist (f/m/d).

YOUR JOB

In the role, you will be responsible for making data from analytical instruments and the LIMS sample database available to scientists in Spotfire. You will help create analytical solutions to provide new insights into our instrument data, and by combining data sources you will help create predictions.
Your other tasks include:
&amp;nbsp;

  • Designing, developing, and maintaining user-friendly data visualizations and dashboards using datasets from various data sources.
  • Implement best practices to ensure dashboard design consistency for the best user experience across projects.
  • Gather user requirements and implement analytical solutions using TERR, Iron Python, and JavaScript.
  • Debugging applications, tracing code, and fixing bugs.
  • Testing and documenting your code.
  • Creating training material and delivering end-user training.
  • Providing technical support to users on a daily basis.
  • Manage access rights to dashboards based on roles and projects.

WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

  • Bachelor&amp;rsquo;s degree in statistics, math, engineering, business, or finance, along with at least two years of relevant experience
  • Strong knowledge and hands-on experience with SQL
  • Experience with installing, maintaining, and administering Spotfire or similar tool.
  • Experience with database administration, SSIS, Confluence, and Jira Service Management is considered favorable but not a requirement.
