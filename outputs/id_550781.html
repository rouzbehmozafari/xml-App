About us

Immatics combines the discovery of true targets for cancer immunotherapies with the development of the right T cell receptors with the goal of enabling a robust and specific T cell response against these targets. This deep know-how is the foundation for our pipeline of Adoptive Cell Therapies and TCR Bispecifics as well as our partnerships with global leaders in the pharmaceutical industry. Operating from Tuebingen, Munich and Houston, we are committed to delivering the power of T cells and to unlocking new avenues for patients in their fight against cancer. For more detailed information, visit www.immatics.com.

Overview&amp;nbsp;

We are currently seeking an Associate Manager - CMC Projects* to strengthen our CMC Biologics department. You will work in an interdisciplinary environment with colleagues from Germany and Houston (TX).

The CMC Biologics department oversees and manages the CMC development of projects from our own drug development pipeline. You will support the CMC Biologics Project Manager with regard to our external CMC activities related to our TCR Bispecifics (TCER&amp;reg;) Program. The position can be located in Tuebingen, Munich or home based (Germany).

Your mission

Your main responsibilities will include but are not limited to the following tasks:

  • Be part of the enthusiastic Immatics CMC Biologics team
  • Identify CMC project objectives
  • Support the planning, coordination, integration, execution, and closure of complex multidisciplinary CMC development modules
  • Plan, manage and oversee the CMC project budget, control costs including invoice management
  • Monitor project progress and identify risks and opportunities to timeline, budget or scope
  • Ensure adequate plans are in place to mitigate risks and enable opportunities
  • Establish and ensure efficient content and information flow between interdisciplinary teams incl. routine status reporting and project reviews to ensure that project goals meet our vision and strategy
  • Support the review of CMC documents and document management
  • Lead and support the implementation of new processes and process improvement initiatives to optimize operational efficiency

Your profile

You have successfully completed your advanced degree in the field of life sciences, medicine, pharmacy, or a comparable education. 2-3 years of experience in the pharmaceutical or life science industry, CMC, and prior budget responsibility for complex projects is an advantage. In previous roles you demonstrated your ability to drive projects from conception to completion. Strong communication and social skills are equally important as approaching tasks in a structured, reliable, and foresighted manner. Besides, you have good English and German language skills (written and spoken).
