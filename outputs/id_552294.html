PURPOSE OF YOUR JOB

Our Mission
In the Data Science Unit, we are implementing the scientific IT infrastructure to match our future ambitions within the field of data science. The solution includes a LIMS, automated data parsers for simple and analytical instruments, and a data visualization tool. As we are approaching the operational phase, we are now looking to hire a second Scientific Application Specialist (f/m/d) to join the team.

YOUR JOB

  • Supporting users, you will help ensure a smooth roll-out of the platform;
  • by creating, testing, and maintaining data parsers, you will help ensure a high-performing analytics platform.
  • You document your work and create procedures to create a robust, future-proof IT solution.
  • You optimize the platform by listening to users&amp;rsquo; requirement and - together with the LIMS vendor &amp;ndash;configure the solution to fit the business&amp;rsquo; needs.
  • You ensure the platform is running smoothly; and
  • you are responsible for administration of accesses and roles, and other administrative master data.

WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

  • You have a background in IT management, computer science, life science informatics, or similar discipline.
  • You have experience with LIMS and sample management from a CRO, biotech, or pharmaceutical company
  • You communicate clearly in English
  • You have a strategic mindset and enjoy staying up-to-date on new technologies
  • You enjoy implementing new technologies and you like to support other people
  • Good documentation practice is part of your DNA
  • Experience with data visualization, data analysis, and programming is considered an advantage but not a must (e.g., Spotfire, R, Python)
  • Experience from working in a GxP environment is beneficial but not required
