About us

Immatics combines the discovery of true targets for cancer immunotherapies with the development of the right T cell receptors with the goal of enabling a robust and specific T cell response against these targets. This deep know-how is the foundation for our pipeline of Adoptive Cell Therapies and TCR Bispecifics as well as our partnerships with global leaders in the pharmaceutical industry. Operating from Tuebingen, Munich and Houston, we are committed to delivering the power of T cells and to unlocking new avenues for patients in their fight against cancer. For more detailed information, visit www.immatics.com.

Overview

Become part of our Sample &amp;amp; Cell Line Management Team in T&amp;uuml;bingen (Germany) and help to accompany our immunotherapies into the clinic. We are currently seeking a Scientist Sample and Cell Line Management*.

Your mission

Your main tasks will include:

  • Providing scientific advice on the selection of suitable human tissue samples as well as cell lines for the characterization and validation of our targets
  • Acquisition and proper management of selected human tissue samples, considering ethical and legal requirements
  • Procurement of selected cell lines; management of contracts and licenses
  • Supporting the development of our therapeutic approaches by researching tissue- and cell line-relevant topics in scientific literature
  • Participating in meetings with internal and external partners as a peer for planning the validation steps for our targets on their way to the clinic
  • Advancing the expansion of our network of commercial and academic biobank partners: Identify new potential leads and establish long-lasting collaborations as well as manage and nurture existing ones
  • Contributing to the preparation of ethics applications
  • Actively contributing to the optimization of tissue- and cell line-related concepts in our database with the support of our bioinformatics department
  • Managing sample metadata diligently
  • Supporting our clinical trials by overseeing sample receipt and storage as well as managing the relevant documentation

Your profile

You hold a PhD degree in Life Sciences or have been comparably educated and you have proven your scientific excellence. You have in-depth knowledge of histology or pathology or have already worked with various types of human tissues. Knowledge of immunology and/ or oncology is an advantage. Your very good communication skills in English and German as well as excellent organization skills enable you to successfully contribute to interdisciplinary projects. Accurate documentation of workflows and results is of highest importance for you.
