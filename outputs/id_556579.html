Your mission

Your main responsibilities will include but are not limited to the following tasks:

  • You are responsible for clinical trial sample management in a GCP environment
  • You are the main point of contact with Immatics stakeholders and logistics partners, promoting synergies and transparency
  • In addition to your role as sample manager, you are the scientific expert that will represent the integral link between sample management and immunological assays

  • You liaise with external partners and organize remote or on-site training, while ensuring contractual obligations are met
  • You coordinate
    • PBMC sample processing with clinical site
    • Sample processing with external partners (e.g. PBMC isolation)
    • Sample shipment, handling and receipt, from clinical site working with external partners and different departments within Immatics
    • Sample kitting and distribution to external partners
  • You are responsible to have the overview of and to streamline processes across all different sample types
  • You manage/own central documentation tool/ central tracker and thereby keep the overview of expected received &amp;amp; analyzed samples
  • You review all sample-associated sections of clinical trial documents (e.g. laboratory manual, SOPs)
