Manager Business Development (f/m/d)

Permanent employee, Full-time &amp;middot; Martinsried (BY)

PURPOSE OF YOUR JOB

Our &amp;ldquo;Business Development (BD) - Unit is looking for a new colleague, who wants to be part of our team in Martinsried close to Munich (remote possible).
The future position holder ensures further development and increase in sales of Coriolis business activities regarding liquid and/or lyophilized biopharmaceutical formulation development on national and/ or international level.

YOUR JOB

  • Develop and grow sales and business development activities for liquid and/or lyophilized biopharmaceutical formulation development (proteins, nucleic acids, peptides, viruses/Vaccines, VLPs cell &amp;amp; gene therapies) / Develop new business opportunities and demonstrate active customer interaction
    • Identify and qualify prospective customers
    • Build relationships with potential customers and strengthen the relationships with the existing customers
    • Understand the business needs of these companies and use the opportunity to present our services to the customers
    • Proactively develop and lead strategic account-planning activities for individual key accounts
  • Create offers and concepts for customers including
    • Participation in technical / scientific discussion
    • Deep scientific expertise and understanding of the field
  • Leading contract negotiations (IP, liability and payments) and pricing in close cooperation with the customer
  • Act as interface to internal and external stakeholders
    • Proactively and reactively assess customer needs and develop solutions within the company to respond to these needs by coordinating internal resources and involving company personnel
    • Ensure customer satisfaction
    • Ensure satisfaction of project team members and other stakeholders e.g. accounting
  • Initiation, planning, leading and execution of projects to reach strategic company goals
  • Ensure utilization of business units
  • Observation of the global industry and development of new technologies
  • Evaluation of the market opportunities of own products and services
  • Development and maintenance of a tight network to opinion maker and other biotechnological and pharmaceutical companies
  • Strategical planning and execution of sales and marketing activities incl. creation, revision and collection of relevant content in close collaboration with marketing manager
  • Represent Coriolis during customer visits, conferences and roadshows (globally)

WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

  • University degree, preferably in a scientific area (pharmacy, biology, biotechnology, chemistry)
  • Further training in business administration and/or marketing and sales preferred &amp;amp; preferably with an additional MBA in an applied science or engineering field
  • Minimum 5 &amp;ndash; 8 years professional experience in Scientific / Pharma / Biopharmaceutical environment
  • Project management experience - able to juggle multiple competing priorities
  • Experience in lyophilization and liquid formulation development
  • Good team player - an energetic and dynamic relationship builder
  • Excellent communication &amp;amp; presentation skills
  • Good English skills (Business fluent)
  • Willingness to travel

INVEST IN YOURSELF

  • Ownership: All team members are empowered to bring ideas, make decisions and have impact. Participate in our equity program and share in our success.
  • Transparency: Everyone at Coriolis is regularly updated on strategic progress, KPI tracking, product updates and changes.
  • Growth: Not only will you grow in your role, you also have access to our people development budget.
  • Flexibility: Work on your terms, by leveraging our hybrid-office arrangement. No minimums or maximums.

Coriolis Pharma, is a globally operating service provider and one of the world leaders in formulation research and development of (bio)pharmaceutical drugs and vaccines.
We revolutionize the development process of biopharmaceutical drugs by integrating innovative digital formulation approaches from drug development to commercial products.
