From our headquarters in Karlsruhe, Germany, Vanguard Automation has set out to revolutionize photonics packaging and integration. Our portfolio covers the entire range from small-scale prototyping to fully automated solutions for manufacturing. Built upon an internationally unique technology and IP portfolio as well as nearly two decades of experience in industrial automation through our partner companies, our growing customer base is inherently global.
